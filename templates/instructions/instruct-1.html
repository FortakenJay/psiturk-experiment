Welcome to the Robot Learning Assistant Study

Study Overview

In this study, you will work with an AI-powered robot tutor that helps you solve mathematical problems. The robot will ask you questions about calculus (derivatives and integrals) and provide feedback based on your answers.

About the Robot

Your AI Robot Tutor

The robot will:

  • Present you with math questions
  • Provide feedback on your answers
  • Offer helpful resources when needed
  • Adapt to help you learn effectively

Your Task

  • Answer each question as accurately as possible
  • You will have 5 minutes to complete all questions
  • The robot will provide feedback after each answer
  • Focus on learning, not just getting answers right!
